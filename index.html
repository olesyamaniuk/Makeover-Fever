County Story: Merge & Cooking

"Celebrate Christmas with Delicious Merges and Festive Cooking Adventures!"

About the Game

County Story: Merge & Cooking is a heartwarming holiday-themed game that combines merging mechanics with cooking adventures. Step into the charming village of County, where you can merge ingredients, cook festive dishes, and share your culinary creations with friends. Explore seasonal recipes, rebuild the town, and celebrate Christmas with new challenges, puzzles, and seasonal events. Get ready to mix, merge, and serve up holiday cheer in this delightful cooking adventure.

Features & Advantages

  • Festive Merge Gameplay:

    Merge different ingredients, decorations, and gifts to create holiday-themed items, from gingerbread houses to Christmas trees, unlocking new decorations and recipes as you progress.

  • Delicious Christmas Dishes:

    Cook a variety of festive dishes, including turkey dinners, Christmas cookies, and eggnog, to delight your villagers and earn valuable rewards.

Holiday Recipes:

  • Gingerbread Cookies:

    Combine flour, sugar, and spices to create the perfect gingerbread dough. Decorate with frosting and candy to make the cookies come alive in the holiday spirit.

  • Christmas Roast Turkey:

    Merge vegetables, herbs, and seasonings to prepare a hearty roast turkey that brings joy to all your guests. Perfect for holiday feasts.

  • Eggnog Delight:

    Combine milk, sugar, and eggs to whip up a creamy eggnog. Garnish with nutmeg for the perfect winter treat to serve at your festive dinners.

  • Rebuild the Village:

    Restore the festive charm of County town by completing challenges and unlocking new areas filled with holiday decorations, festive lights, and seasonal objects.

  • Christmas-Themed Events:

    Participate in special holiday events, such as the Christmas Cookie Challenge or the Winter Festival, to unlock limited-edition rewards and exclusive festive content.

  • Offline Play:

    Enjoy the holiday magic anywhere without needing an internet connection. Perfect for playing on the go during the Christmas season.

  • Frequent Updates:

    Expect new seasonal updates with new recipes, ingredients, and challenges to keep the holiday cheer going throughout the year.

How to Play

  • 1

    Merge Ingredients:

    Combine everyday items like fruits, vegetables, and baking supplies to create festive dishes, decorations, and gifts.

  • 2

    Cook Delicious Dishes:

    Follow recipes and merge ingredients to cook a variety of Christmas treats and festive meals, delighting your villagers and earning rewards.

  • 3

    Restore the Village:

    Complete puzzles and merge activities to help restore County village to its holiday glory, unlocking new areas and exciting challenges.

  • 4

    Enjoy Special Holiday Quests:

    Participate in limited-time quests that allow you to earn exclusive holiday items and rewards, enhancing your festive adventure.
