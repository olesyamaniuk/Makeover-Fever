About the Game

In Makeover Fever, players become makeover experts, helping clients achieve their dream looks! Create stunning makeovers, tackle unique challenges, and unlock fabulous beauty tools. Whether it’s a new hairstyle, makeup design, or a stylish wardrobe, every choice transforms your client’s confidence. With colorful visuals, addictive gameplay, and endless customization options, Makeover Fever is the ultimate beauty adventure.
